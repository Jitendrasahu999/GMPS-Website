Who We Are

Welcome To Great Mission Public School

The School Previously Known As Maria Montessori Happy Home Was Founded In The Year 1978 At Ramnagar Started In A Rented Building.

The Founder Of The School Late Mr. K.B.L. Srivastava, A Psychologist Be Educationist, Who Would Devote His Live To The Cause Of Education. And So, In The Year 1984 The Org. GREAT MISSION EDUCATION SOCIETY OF INDIA Came Into Being Happy Home Was Renamed As GREAT MISSION PUBLIC SCHOOL.

GREAT MISSION PUBLIC SCHOOL, Himmatpur Block, Ramnagar (Ntl.) Stands On Main Road In Between Ramnagar & Kashipur 10 To 15 Minutes Ride From Both The Places.

Our Core Values

Creative Activities

At our school, creativity blooms! Through art, music, dance, and hands-on projects, we nurture every child's imagination and self-expression.

Happy Environment

We believe a happy child is a thriving learner. Our school fosters a joyful, caring, and inclusive environment where every child feels safe and valued.

Amazing Playground

Our spacious and vibrant playground is every child’s favorite place! It’s designed for fun, fitness, and friendship, encouraging active play and teamwork.

Active Learning

Learning comes alive in our classrooms! With interactive lessons, practical experiences, and student engagement at the core, we make learning exciting and meaningful.
